Multivariate Data Analysis Assignment 3

Introduction

In this report, we are asked to analyse a dataset and present our findings.

The data that we analyse for this report is mRNA gene expression data, from 60 samples and across 2690 genes from a hypothetical (simulated) study.

The aims of this simulated study are to analyse the effects of two treatments on a cell culture. The 60 samples are split across two covariates: time (6 or 12 hours) and treatment (lisinopril, amlodipine, or control).

Our workflow first presents high-level qualities of the data. We move onto Principal Components Analysis (PCA) and Cluster Analysis, finding meaningful partitions of the data. We fit a Linear Discriminant Rule (LDR) to the data, and complete the analysis with a brief application of graph-based clustering to the same data.

Plot of Variation Explained per PC
